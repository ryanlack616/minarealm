Online Shop

Shop the Mineral Realm

Crystals, jewelry, fossils, and spiritual tools — available for local pickup in Fenton & Hartland or shipping across the US. Orders are confirmed by email; payment collected via Square invoice.

Ethically sourced

Every crystal, fossil, and stone is hand-selected from trusted suppliers and small-scale dealers we know by name. We favor responsibly mined and reclaimed pieces — and we'll always share what we know about where something came from. Just ask.

Shipping

Orders ship USPS from Michigan, usually within 2–3 business days. Flat-rate shipping is calculated by weight at checkout, and orders over $100 ship free. Local pickup in Fenton & Hartland is always free — just choose pickup at checkout.

Returns & care

Natural stones vary in color, shape, and inclusions — that's part of their character. If something arrives damaged or isn't right, reach out within 14 days and we'll make it right with a replacement or refund: info@minarealm.shop.

Secure checkout

Payment is handled by Square on their secure, PCI-compliant pages — we never see or store your card details. You'll receive an emailed confirmation for every order.
