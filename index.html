Booking & Cancellation Policy
Walk-ins & Appointments: Walk-ins are always welcome for shopping during normal business hours. For readings and spiritual services, appointments are highly recommended and receive priority — same-day walk-in service requests are accommodated based on availability. Classes, workshops, and special events require advance registration due to limited spots.

Cancellations & Deposits: We require at least 24 hours' notice for all cancellations. Some service providers require a deposit or upfront payment for specific events — these are non-refundable if canceled without 24-hour notice. In some instances, a credit may be offered toward a future session at the sole discretion of the individual service provider.

Late Arrivals: Sessions are scheduled to start promptly. If you arrive late, we will do our very best to accommodate you; however, your session may be shortened to stay on schedule for other clients.

No-Shows: We are happy to reschedule no-shows, but we require full payment upfront to book a new date with our service providers. This payment becomes non-refundable if the new appointment is canceled without 24 hours' notice.

Welcome to Minarealm Crystals - a metaphysical sanctuary built on a lifelong passion for crystals, fossils, and the natural world.
