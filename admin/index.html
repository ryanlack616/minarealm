How saving & images work

Auto-save: retail price, wholesale cost, stock, and product edits save automatically to the cloud after a brief pause. The status badge in the top-right shows Saving… → Saved ✓.

Margin tracking: set both Retail and Wholesale to unlock per-item margin and portfolio gross-profit KPIs in Dashboard.

Images: drag a photo onto the dropzone (or tap to choose). It uploads to the cloud and the URL is filled in automatically. The first image is the cover; reorder with the ↑ ↓ buttons.

Orders: the Orders tab shows incoming customer orders. Mark them Fulfilled when shipped/picked up. New orders also email cynthia@minarealm.org.

These submissions are stored internally in the Worker KV, not sent through Formspree.
